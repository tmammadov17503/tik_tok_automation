Film Box Official

TikTok video automation workspace

Film Box Official is a desktop workflow for preparing, scheduling, and publishing short-form videos to an authorized TikTok account.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

What it does

The app helps the account owner generate vertical clips, add captions, queue approved posts, and publish them through official TikTok developer tools.

What it does not do

The service is intended only for content that the user owns or has permission to edit and publish. Unauthorized reposting is not supported.

Contact

For support or account questions, contact the operator of Film Box Official through the linked TikTok profile or the repository associated with this app.
