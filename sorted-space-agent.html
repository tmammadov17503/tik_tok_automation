Sorted Space Agent

Sorted Space Agent is a private workflow for creating and scheduling original Pinterest Pins for The Sorted Space business account.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

What it does

The app helps the account owner turn approved Amazon Associates product links into original Pin images, titles, descriptions, affiliate disclosures, and scheduled posts on authorized Pinterest boards.

Account access

The app uses Pinterest API authorization only after the account owner grants permission. It does not request or store Pinterest passwords.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact the operator through the GitHub repository associated with this app.
