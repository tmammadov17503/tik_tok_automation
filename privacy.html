Film Box Official

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Overview

Film Box Official is a desktop automation tool that helps an authorized user prepare and publish short-form video content to TikTok. This policy explains what information may be processed when the user connects their TikTok account and uses the app.

Information we process

Depending on the connected TikTok permissions, the app may process:

  • basic TikTok account identifiers such as open ID, display name, and avatar
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens required to keep the user connected
  • video files, captions, metadata, and posting schedules created by the user
  • technical logs needed to troubleshoot failed uploads or publishing jobs

How information is used

  • to authenticate the user with TikTok
  • to upload, queue, and publish content that the user has approved
  • to maintain scheduling, status tracking, and delivery logs
  • to improve reliability, error handling, and support

Storage

Tokens, local media files, and workflow data may be stored on the user's own computer or deployment environment. The app operator should keep secrets in protected local configuration files and remove local media when it is no longer needed.

Sharing

Information is shared only as needed to interact with TikTok's official APIs and any infrastructure explicitly configured by the user for storage, hosting, or automation.

User responsibilities

Users must ensure they have the legal right to upload, edit, schedule, and publish the content they process through this app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Film Box Official app operator through the same support channel listed on the app homepage or repository.
